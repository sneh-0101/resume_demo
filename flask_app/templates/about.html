About AI Resume Analyzer

Our Mission

We believe job seekers deserve intelligent tools to optimize their applications. AI Resume Analyzer helps you understand how your resume matches job requirements, identify gaps, and get actionable recommendations for improvement.

Technology

Our platform uses advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning to:

  • Extract Resume Data: Intelligent PDF parsing to identify your skills, experience, and education
  • Analyze Job Requirements: NLP processing to extract key skills and qualifications
  • Calculate Match Scores: Hybrid algorithm using TF-IDF and skill-based matching
  • Generate Insights: AI-powered suggestions for resume optimization
How It Works
  1. Upload Your Resume: Upload your PDF resume securely
  2. Paste Job Description: Provide the job posting you're interested in
  3. Get Analysis: Receive instant AI-powered analysis
  4. Review Results: See your match score and improvement suggestions
  5. Download Report: Get a professional PDF report
Privacy & Security

Your data is important to us. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices:

  • ✅ All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • ✅ Password hashing with strong algorithms
  • ✅ No data sharing with third parties
  • ✅ Only you can access your resumes and analyses
  • ✅ GDPR compliant data handling
